Privacy Policy

GrainSight · Effective Date: April 28, 2026
Plain English summary: GrainSight is a woodworking shop management app. We collect only the information needed to make the app work. We don't sell your data, we don't show you ads, and we try to be straightforward about what we store and why.

1. Who We Are

GrainSight is developed and operated by Andrew Jones, an individual developer based in Custer Park, Illinois, United States.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at flyguy3166@gmail.com.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use GrainSight:

Account Information
  • Email address and password (used for account creation and login via Supabase Auth)
  • Your name and business/workshop name (entered during onboarding)
Workshop Data
  • Lumber inventory records (species, dimensions, cost, photos)
  • Project details, cut lists, and material assignments
  • Client contact information you enter manually
  • Quotes, line items, tax settings, and pricing preferences
  • Workshop zones and storage locations
Photos & Images
  • Photos of lumber boards taken within the app
  • Your company logo if you choose to upload one
  • Images are stored securely in Supabase Storage
Usage & Technical Data
  • App settings and preferences
  • Error logs to help us fix bugs (no personal information included)
  • Subscription status and billing information (processed by Stripe — we do not store card numbers)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the GrainSight app and its features
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Sync your workshop data across your devices
  • Process subscription payments through Stripe
  • Send account-related emails (confirmation, password reset) — no marketing emails
  • Improve the app based on how it is used (aggregated, non-personal data only)
  • Respond to support requests you send us

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than operating GrainSight.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with the following service providers who help us operate the app:

  • Supabase — database and file storage (supabase.com)
  • Railway — backend server hosting (railway.app)
  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com). Stripe handles all payment card data and is PCI compliant. We never see or store your full card number.
  • Expo / EAS — app distribution and updates (expo.dev)

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety and rights of users and the public.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on servers in the United States. We use industry-standard security practices including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit
  • Supabase Row Level Security to ensure users can only access their own data
  • Secure authentication via Supabase Auth

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Photos & Camera Access

GrainSight requests access to your device camera and photo library to allow you to:

  • Take photos of lumber boards for inventory tracking
  • Use AI-assisted measurement features with ArUco markers
  • Upload a company logo

Photos are only accessed when you actively use these features. They are uploaded to your private storage and are not shared with other users or third parties.

You can revoke camera and photo library permissions at any time in your device Settings.

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — You can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — You can update your information directly in the app or by contacting us
  • Deletion — You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at flyguy3166@gmail.com
  • Portability — You can export your data by contacting us
  • Opt out — You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at flyguy3166@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

GrainSight is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Subscriptions & Billing

GrainSight offers a free trial period followed by paid subscription plans. Payments are processed by Stripe. We store your subscription status and Stripe customer ID, but never store payment card details.

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by email. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect when the policy was last updated.

Continued use of GrainSight after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your California privacy rights, contact us at flyguy3166@gmail.com.
